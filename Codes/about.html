About RwandaFix

RwandaFix is a smart digital platform developed to help citizens report damaged roads, broken water pipes, electricity failures, flooding, garbage problems, broken street lights and other public infrastructure issues across Rwanda. The platform encourages collaboration between citizens and local authorities to improve public services and create safer communities.
