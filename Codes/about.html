About RwandaFix

RwandaFix is a modern digital platform designed to help citizens report damaged roads, water pipe leaks, electricity failures and other infrastructure problems across Rwanda.

Our Vision

To build a cleaner, safer and smarter Rwanda through technology, innovation and community participation.

Our Mission

To connect citizens with authorities using a simple, fast and reliable digital reporting platform.
